Purpose:

This website is intended to enhance communcation between you and your advocate.

How to Navigate Webpage Tabs:

Safety Plan: Please fillout an assessment form at any point.
This assessment is intended to measure the level of risk in your relationship. The assessment will be sent to your advocate who will review the results in order to understand how to better support and help you. If the assessment has a high score, you will have an option to send the results to Clearwater Police Department as well as your advocate

Financial Options: You can fillout your Victim Compensation Application within the Financial tab.
The results will be sent to your advocate who will then send the applicaiton to the Office of Attorney General.

Submissions: You can check the status of any of the forms you have submitted.

Shelter: You may check the status of Haven's 24/hour emergency shelter at any time.
Please contact your advocate or our 24 hour emergency hotline: 1-800-799-7233 if there are available beds and you feel you qualify for Shelter

Legal Advocacy: By accessing the legal advocacy tab you are able to find a defendant's criminal history. Please contact your advocate for further legal resources.

Message: Access the Messaging tab to communicate with your advocate online. You may contact your advocate at any time.
